About Lydia

Hi. I'm Lydia. I'm a Boston-based student, artist, and pug-lover.

My love for calligraphy began as the result of my inability to pay attention in class. Though I've been making things for people since high school, I "officially" began Callydgraphy November of 2015 while I was recovering from my ACL surgery. Since then, I've been able to work on a wide variety of commissions, ranging from seating charts on mirrors, wedding programs on chalkboards, personal greeting cards, wedding invitations, vow books, and more. It's been a blast so far, and I look forward to helping you put to paper (or mirror or chalkboard or whatever) exactly what you want!

Contact: lydia.grasso@gmail.com
